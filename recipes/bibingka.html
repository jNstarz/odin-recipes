Steps

  1. Cut circles from banana leaves to line up pans. Cut another batch for top cover, then rinse and pat dry the leaves
  2. Line pans with banana leaves and brush with butter
  3. Combine ingredients for batter in a bowl and mix well until smooth and runny
  4. Divide mixture into molds and add 3 slices of salted eggs.
  5. Arrange on baking sheet and bake at 200 degrees Celsius for 10 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and top Bibingka with butter, cheese and coconut
