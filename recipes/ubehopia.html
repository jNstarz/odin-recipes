Ube Hopia

Ube hopia is an ube variant of the Filipino snack .

Ingredients

  • Red mung bean
  • Condensed milk
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Ube halaya
  • Shortening
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Soak mung beans overnight to prepare for munggo filling
  2. Boil until softened, add sugar and cook until it thickens
  3. Put into blender until it becomes smooth, adding condensed milk and flour while cooking
  4. Combine ingredients for first dough and mix until shortening is cut into small pieces
  5. Knead until it becomes sticky, cover it and set aside
  6. Combine ingredients from second dough
  7. Roll first dough into rectangle and a half, then roll second dough about 2/3 the size of first dough
  8. Fold dough to make three layers
  9. Roll to original size and do three-fold. Let dough rest for a few minutes
  10. Roll dough and cut into half. Cut into 12 equal parts
  11. Flatten dough as thin as possible, then pour munggo folling in the middle
  12. Take round cookie cutter and put dough inside, pinched side down
  13. Place hopia on baking hseet and bake at 180 degrees Celsus for 20 minutes
  14. Take out from oven and brush with egg wash, then bake for another 10 minutes
  15. Remove from oven, let it cool and enjoy
